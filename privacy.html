The Coding Samurai website is operated by Coding Samurai, which acts as the data controller for your personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we process the data collected by Coding Samurai and explains the reasons for collecting specific personal data about you. We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy thoroughly before using our website.

You can browse our website without disclosing your identity or revealing any identifiable personal information. However, to access certain features or receive our newsletter, you may need to provide personal data, such as your email, first name, last name, city of residence, organization, and telephone number. You can choose not to provide personal data, but doing so may limit your ability to utilize certain website features, such as receiving our newsletter or contacting us directly. If you’re uncertain about what information is mandatory, please feel free to reach out to us at contact@codingsamurai.in.

Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the Coding Samurai website, we automatically gather certain information about your device, which includes details about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and cookies installed on your device. As you navigate our site, we also collect information about the specific web pages or products you view, the websites or search terms that referred you to our site, and your interactions with it. This automatically collected information is referred to as “Device Information.” Additionally, we may collect personal data you provide during registration to fulfill our agreements, which may include your name, address, payment information, and more.

Why We Process Your Data

Our primary focus is on the security of customer data. Therefore, we only process the minimal amount of user data necessary to maintain our website. The information collected automatically helps us identify potential abuse and compile statistical data regarding website usage. This statistical information does not identify individual users.

You can browse our website without disclosing your identity.

Your Rights

If you are a resident of Europe, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • The right to be informed.
  • The right of access.
  • The right to rectification.
  • The right to erasure.
  • The right to restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object.
  • Rights concerning automated decision-making and profiling.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Additionally, for European residents, we process your information to fulfill contracts we may have with you (e.g., if you place an order through our site) or to pursue our legitimate business interests. Please note that your information may be transferred outside of Europe, including to Canada and the United States.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may include links to other websites not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites or third parties. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of any website you visit after leaving our site.

Information Security

We secure the information you provide on computer servers located in a controlled and secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, use, modification, and disclosure of your personal data. However, please be aware that no data transmission over the Internet or wireless network can be completely guaranteed.

Legal Disclosure

We will disclose any information we collect, use, or receive when required or permitted by law, such as in response to a subpoena or similar legal process, and when we believe, in good faith, that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to governmental requests.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to discuss matters related to your individual rights and personal information, please contact us at contact@codingsamurai.in.
